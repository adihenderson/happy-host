The Happy Host

Hello, and welcome to The Happy Host! We are lifelong best friends who live in two different tourist hotspots in the Western United States. One of us lives within a mile of gorgeous beaches on the golden coast of California with towering eucalyptus trees in the backyard. The other in the deeply forested northwest 15 minutes from a ski resort and 5 minutes to a huge lakeside beach. And though we are seperated by considerable distance we have been talking nearly every week for the last 23 years and travel together every year. Our family and friends love to visit us and over the years we have learned a lot about what makes for a pleasant trip, for both the host and the house guest.
